De Cairo's Mountain & Ocean View Resort in Boljoon, Cebu,was created on June 18, 2018 the name of the resort was inspired by there last name which is Cairo thats why the name of there resort is De Cairo's the owner of De Cairo's Resort is Jun Jun Cairo, it offers a tranquil retreat with stunning mountain and ocean views. Boljoon boasts rich cultural heritage, historic churches, and well-preserved colonial architecture, including the UNESCO World Heritage Site candidate, Boljoon Church.

The owner of De Cairo Mountain Resort is a man shaped by nature and guided by purpose. Raised with simple values and a deep love for the outdoors, he spent his early years exploring mountains, forests, and rivers. His travels across continents strengthened his belief that peace, beauty, and silence are life’s greatest luxuries.
